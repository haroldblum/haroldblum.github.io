I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Previously, I was an assistant professor at the University of Utah and an NSF postdoc at Stony Brook University and the University of Utah. I completed my Ph.D. in 2018 at the University of Michigan under the supervision of Mircea Mustață.

My research is in algebraic geometry with a focus on birational geometry, K-stability, Fano varieties, Calabi-Yau varieties, and moduli.

My work is partially supported by NSF CAREER Grant DMS-2441378 and NSF Grant DMS-2200690.
